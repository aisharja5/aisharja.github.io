What is an Apple?

An apple is an edible fruit produced by an apple tree. Apple trees are cultivated worldwide and are the most widely grown species in the . The tree originated in Central Asia.There are more than 7,500 known cultivars of apples. Different cultivars are bred for various tastes and use, including cooking, eating raw and cider production.

What can an apple be used for?

An apple can be eaten raw or it can be used when cooking and/or baking. Apple can also be used to make drinks, such as apple juice.
